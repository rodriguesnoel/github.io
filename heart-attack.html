Will I Get A Heart Attack

Python Data Analysis

Overview

A classic binary prediction if a patient is at risk of a heart attack based on standard health variables.

  • The dataset gathered from Kaggle contained variables such age, sex, blood pressure, cholesterol level, etc.
Technologies Used
  • Libraries used were Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Scikit, and Seaborn.
  • The data was trained on Logistics Regression, Random Forest, and Decision Tree.
  • The model was then tested and predicted on Logistics Regression.
Key Findings
  • Logistics Regression gives the best accuracy compared to other models.
  • Exercise-induced angina and chest pain are major symptoms of heart attack.
  • Based on the parameters and my cardiological report, my risk of getting a heart attack is low.
