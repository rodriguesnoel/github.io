Who Will Win the English Premier League

Excel & SQL Analysis

Overview

Data science is integral to all NBA clubs. Will it help an English Premier League club win the league?

  • Every professional sport club is using data science to optimise their chances in winning.
  • This project attempts to prove its efficacy with data gathered from Google Dataset Search and Kaggle.
Technologies Used
  • SQL was employed for data cleaning, and exported to Excel for data visualisation and evaluation.
Key Findings
  • Expensive, big-money players, to a large extent, do win silverware, but it does not guarantee success.
  • A superior playing style to adopt is a more offensive play, accurate shooting, and a more pressing adoption.
  • West Ham players need to improve on their shots at goals, headers, accurate passing and effective dribbles.
  • With these changes, West Ham will significantly improve their table position of 16th to a higher table position.
